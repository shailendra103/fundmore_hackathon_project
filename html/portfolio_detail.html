Investment Summary

The Opportunity Provider has acquired a piece of land to build a 41-unit condominium project in Seremban. The total amount required from the Opportunity Provider is to build the project is $10,000,000, The Opportunity Provider is using its internal funds of $5,000,000 to finance the projects construction and it needs to raise the balance 50% - 5,000,000 using debt financing. It is expected that construction will take one and a half years while marketing and selling the project will take another one and a half years. Hence, the opportunity provider is confident of repaying the loans to investors after 3 years.

The property is located in downtown Seremban, which is the 4th most populous metropolitan area in the nation. Seremban also has the third-largest manufacturing employment market in the country and has experienced strong growth over the last 5 years. According the the recent 1Q16 market report by WTW, the Seremban residential market has a vacancy rate of approximately 4.5% with rent growth of nearly 8% year on year.

The Opportunity Provider, ABC Sdn Bhd, is a real estate development and construction firm focused on selected Malaysian residential markets including Seremban and KL.
